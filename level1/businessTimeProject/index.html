Fiddler's Green Coffee

Halfway down the trail to hell, in a shady meadow green, are the souls of all dead troopers camped near a good ol' time canteen, and this eternal resting place is known as Fiddler's Green.

Marching passed, straight through to hell, the Infantry are seen, accompanied by the Engineers, Artillery, and Marines. For none but the shades of Cavalry men dismount at Fiddler's Green.

Though some go marching down the trail to seek a warmer scene, no trooper ever gets to hell 'ere he's emptied his canteen, and so rides back to drink again with friends at Fiddler's Green.

And so when man and horse go down beneath the saber keen, or in a roaring charge of fierce melee, you stop a bullet clean, and the hostiles come to get your scalp, just empty your canteen, and put your pistol to your head, and go to Fiddler's Green.
